Indicator Dashboard

RSI (14-day)
…
Momentum gauge. Above 70 is often called "overbought," below 30 "oversold." It's a signal, not a certainty.
MACD (12/26/9)
…
Tracks momentum shifts. When the MACD line crosses above its signal line it's read as building upward momentum — and the reverse for downward.
Bollinger %B (20-day)
…
Where price sits inside its volatility band. Near 100% hugs the top, near 0% hugs the bottom, below 0 or above 1 means price has pushed outside the band.
Trend (50 vs 200-day avg)
…
When the short-term average sits above the long-term one, the trend is generally read as upward — and vice versa.
vs 50-day average
…
How far today's price sits above or below its 50-day average — a rough read on short-term strength.
Volume (vs 20-day avg)
…
How today's trading volume compares to its recent average. A spike means a move is backed by real participation rather than a thin drift.
