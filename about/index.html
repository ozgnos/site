About Ozgnos

The who, the why, and the rules we hold ourselves to.

What Ozgnos is

Ozgnos is an independent crypto education site. It exists to do one thing well: explain crypto in plain English — what things are, how they work, and how to stay safe — without the hype, the jargon, or a hidden sales pitch.

No venture capital, no exchange behind it, no token to pump. Just lessons, live market data presented as education, and a free newsletter.

Who's behind this

Ozgnos is a one-person project — no exchange, no fund, no boss. It has watched a few cycles come and go: the euphoria, the crashes, the influencers who vanish when the chart turns red. None of it is new, and that calm is the whole point. Most crypto content is written by people who profit when you're confused — exchanges selling volume, influencers selling their bags, courses selling shortcuts. Ozgnos sells none of that. The goal is simple: help you understand this well enough to know when to say no.

— Seth

Why trust us over an exchange's academy?

Because we don't make money when you trade. An exchange's learning hub exists, ultimately, to turn readers into traders. Ozgnos has no trading product to sell you — which means we can tell you when not to do something, and we often will.

The rules we hold ourselves to

  • Education, never advice. Nothing on this site tells you to buy or sell anything. Ever.
  • A clear line between fact and take. When something is our opinion, we say so.
  • Sources you can check. We'd rather you verify than believe.
  • Full transparency on money. If we ever recommend a product through a referral link, we'll say so right where the link is — and a referral will never change what we write.
  • Honest about risk. Crypto can go to zero. Anyone who skips that part is selling you something.
